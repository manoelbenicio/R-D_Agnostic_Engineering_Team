## Implementation Details
As W5 has not yet published the correlation API contract (`internal/daemon/observability/e2e`), W3 has started and completed its independent work by stubbing the required calls.

1. **8.2 and 8.3**: Previously implemented synthetically in G4; verified that native adapters for Kimi, GLM, NVIDIA, NIM, and Agy remain deterministic fail-closed. No credential or secret data is read, copied, or printed.
2. **OBS-5 CLI-process span**: Added calls to `e2e.StartCLIProcessSpan` in `pkg/agent/claude.go` and `pkg/agent/codex.go`.
   - **Launch and Exit**: Hooked immediately after `cmd.Start()` and wrapped `cmd.Wait()` to capture exit.
   - **Exit Code**: Retrieved from `cmd.ProcessState.ExitCode()`.
   - **Cancellation**: Traced via `runCtx.Err()`.
   - **Structurally-redacted argv**: Generated by `safeAgentArgvForLog` (shape only, never values).
   - **Join ID**: A new UUID is generated as `launchID` and passed along with `cmd.Process.Pid` to the e2e library stub.
